Driving License Notifications To Your Phone.

Serving Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach and Orange County Residents

The Issue

  • Almost 2 Million Floridians have their license suspended.[1]
  • In 2017, over 1.1 million suspension notices, making up nearly 72% of all suspension notices that year, were for unpaid fines and fees, not for dangerous driving or public safety concerns. [2]

Our Mission

Our mission is to help users stay informed of their driving record with proactive updates and reports.

How it works

We use the various counties Clerk Of Courts public API's to run reports using the provided information including the date of birth and the drivers license. Once an update is available a summary will be sent via SMS (please note charges may apply) to the number provided. These reports are run usually monthly per record.

User data

We take our users data security very seriously and use encryption of data and communication.

Cost

Currently this service is free to use, limited to one driver's license per person. If you would like to donate or need to subscribe to multiple driver licenses please email support@drivefine.com
